## Re-enabling Disabled Providers (Optional)

### To Re-enable Together AI:
1. Go to https://api.together.ai/settings/billing
2. Add credits to your account
3. Update `.env`:
   ```env
   TOGETHER_API_KEY=your_new_api_key_here
   ```
4. Restart AI service:
   ```bash
   cd ai-service
   python main.py
   ```

### To Re-enable DeepSeek:
1. Add credits to your DeepSeek account
2. Update `.env`:
   ```env
   DEEPSEEK_API_KEY=your_api_key_here
   ```
3. Restart AI service

**Note:** Service works perfectly with just Groq and Gemini. Re-enabling other providers is optional.
